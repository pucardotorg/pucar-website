The Litigant's Journey

What actually happens to a bounced cheque in court.

Follow a single §138 Negotiable Instruments Act complaint, from the moment a cheque is dishonoured to the day the respondent stands before a judge. Every hand it passes through, every place it can stall, and every fork in the road. Mapped in Kerala, where e-filing and the Case Information System are integrated.
