Bharat Chugh

Advocate and Founder, The Chambers of Bharat Chugh · The Chambers of Bharat Chugh

Bharat is a former judge of the Delhi Judicial Service who now practises as arguing counsel, leading a team of over a dozen lawyers at The Chambers of Bharat Chugh in Delhi and Gurgaon. He was earlier a Partner at L&L Partners and is a Registered Foreign Lawyer at the Singapore International Commercial Court. He lectures widely, from the National Police Academy to judicial academies, and writes on justice and law.
