Vikas Mahendra

Co-founder, CORD; Joint Secretary, Arbitration Bar of India

Vikas is a co-founder of CORD (Centre for Online Resolution of Disputes) and Joint Secretary of the Arbitration Bar of India, now building AI for complex disputes. A construction disputes specialist, he spent six years at Herbert Smith Freehills across London, Paris, and Singapore, and nine years as Partner at Keystone Partners in Bengaluru. He also advises TERES and co-chairs the Campaign for Greener Arbitrations.
