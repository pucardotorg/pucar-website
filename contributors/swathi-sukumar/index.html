Swathi Sukumar

Senior Advocate, Delhi High Court · Advocate, Delhi High Court

Swathi is a Senior Advocate at the Delhi High Court, designated in 2024, and one of India's leading intellectual property litigators. She is co-founder of iProbono India, which has connected thousands of people to pro bono legal help since 2010, and a door tenant at Hogarth Chambers in London. Her TEDx talk asks why lawyers in India should do pro bono work.
