Rangin Pallav Tripathy

Professor of Law, National Law University Odisha · NLU Odisha

Rangin is a Professor of Law at National Law University Odisha, where he has taught since 2010. A Fulbright Scholar at Harvard Law School, his research focuses on judicial accountability, transparency, and the empirical study of India's courts. He describes himself simply as a researcher and teacher.
