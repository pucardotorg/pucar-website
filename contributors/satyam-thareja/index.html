Satyam Thareja

Advocate-on-Record, Supreme Court of India · TNC

Satyam is an Advocate-on-Record at the Supreme Court of India and founding partner of TNC in New Delhi. He earlier practised in the chambers of senior advocate Sidharth Luthra and served as law clerk and research assistant to Justice R. M. Lodha, former Chief Justice of India. He has practised law since 2012.
