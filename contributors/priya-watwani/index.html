Priya Watwani

Human Rights Lawyer; MPP candidate, LSE · iProbono

Priya is a human rights lawyer and an advocate with the Bar Council of Delhi since 2016. She spent over six years at iProbono, most recently as Senior Program Officer, connecting people who need legal help with lawyers willing to act pro bono. A Lady Meherbai D Tata Scholar, she is currently pursuing her Master of Public Policy at the London School of Economics.
