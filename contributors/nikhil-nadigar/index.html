Nikhil Nadiger

Co-founder, theStaffRoom · Samagra

Nikhil was Vice President at Samagra, where as Programme Lead for PUCAR he led a 12-member team working with the judiciary in Karnataka and Kerala and the Supreme Court eCommittee to conceptualise, design, and build DRISTI, the open-source court technology behind filing, summons, hearings, and orders. He has since co-founded theStaffRoom, a platform and podcast for India's teachers.
