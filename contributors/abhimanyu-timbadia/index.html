Abhimanyu Timbadia

Strategy, Founder’s Office at Alt Carbon · Samagra

Abhimanyu worked on PUCAR through Samagra, where he spent two years on governance programmes ranging from courtroom processes to open-source communities and agricultural systems serving over 90 lakh farmers. He has since joined the founder's office at Alt Carbon, a carbon removal startup, and is starting his MBA at IIM Bangalore. A gold medalist from Ashoka University, he writes regularly on technology and consumer culture.
